Let's Go

Klean Kanteen

Klean Kanteen bottles are made from durable metal and, depending on the style you choose, may or may not be insulated.

Klean Kanteens range in price with non-insulated bottles having an average price of about $22 and insulated bottles costing about $32.

Kanteens come in a large variety of colors although, unlike S'well, they do not offer patterned bottles.
